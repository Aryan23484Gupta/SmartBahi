About SmartBahi

Welcome to SmartBahi – your simple and smart solution for managing personal and small business financial transactions. Whether you lend money to a friend, borrow from someone, or track daily expenses, SmartBahi helps you stay organized and worry-free.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make financial tracking easy, accessible, and efficient for everyone — especially small business owners, shopkeepers, and individuals who still rely on traditional methods like paper registers or handwritten bahi-khatas.

Why SmartBahi?

  • 📊 Track money you give or take — anytime, anywhere.
  • 🔔 Get reminders and notifications for due payments.
  • 🔐 Secure and private — your data is safe with us.
  • 📱 Easy-to-use interface for all age groups.

Who Is It For?

  • ✔️ Shopkeepers & Local Businesses
  • ✔️ Friends managing shared expenses
  • ✔️ Individuals keeping track of personal loans
  • ✔️ Anyone tired of manual accounting

Our Vision

We envision a future where every individual has control over their finances — without the need for complex tools or financial knowledge. With SmartBahi, we're bringing the power of smart digital ledgers to your fingertips.

Thank you for choosing SmartBahi – where smart people manage money the smart way!
